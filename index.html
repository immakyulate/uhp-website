About · The founder

Meet Steven.

The story behind UHP — and why every system inside it exists.

Steven Nhan · Est. 2019 12 wk Self-transformation 500+ Clients coached
Founder· Coach· Business owner· Dad

Hi, I'm Steven Nhan — the founder of The Ultimate Health Project.

I know what it's like to push hard in business while putting your health on the back burner. In my early 20s, I was diagnosed with rheumatoid arthritis. Within weeks, I went from feeling unstoppable to struggling with simple movements. That experience forced me to rethink everything.

What I discovered changed my life and eventually became the foundation of UHP.

I rebuilt my health through smart nutrition, targeted supplementation, and simple, repeatable habits that actually fit into a busy life. Within 12 weeks, I had my energy back — and more clarity and focus than I'd had in years.

Today, I coach business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals who want to feel sharper, leaner, and more in control of their health — without restrictive diets or burnout.

At The Ultimate Health Project, we create personalised systems that are practical, sustainable, and aligned with your lifestyle so you can perform at your best, long term.

The result I'm most proud of

Helping my dad, nearly 70, maintain under 15% body fat and great energy year-round — proof that with the right approach, anyone can take control of their health.

If you're ready to lead with more energy and confidence — without sacrificing your business or lifestyle — I'd love to support you.
